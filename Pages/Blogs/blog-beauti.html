An Overview of Portrait Techniques,
Both in the Studio and Outdoors,
and How to Build Relationships with Your Subjects

Portrait photography combines studio and outdoor techniques with building trust and connection, allowing the subject's personality to shine in every shot.

Introduction

Portrait photography is an art that goes beyond capturing a person's appearance. Whether in a controlled studio setting or natural outdoor environment, mastering lighting, composition, and angles is essential to create striking and expressive images.

Equally important is building a genuine connection with your subjects. Making them feel comfortable and confident allows their true personality and emotions to come through, resulting in portraits that are not only visually appealing but also deeply authentic.
